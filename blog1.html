RESPONSE TO DAILY MAIL'S POST

I really wish I'm home in this great mourning and transitional time. It's important to remember that underneath all the nightlife and wild sexual attractions, Thailand is a country that is deeply cultural, and spiritual oriented. Before wearing a tight neon tank top, remember that we are a country that wear and decorate our body with traditional garments. Before reacting a scene from HANGOVER II, remember our value on being grounded to self (พอเพียง). We ain't America that pride itself for its diversity. Thais are united through our deeply rooted cultural and spiritual bonds. We smile cuz we know we are exactly where we need to be. Our king taught us that and so much more. He stood for everything that is Thai, and now our beacon is gone. Please know that Thailand won't be the 'land of smile' for a while and for a little while, gives us time to rebuild, reconnect and rediscover what it means to be Thai.
